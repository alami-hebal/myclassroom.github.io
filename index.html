WHAT IS THIS WEB PAGE ABOUT?

This web page develop to built online class environment to survey student behavior and activity during online learning. This will involves setting up systems to monitor, record, and analyze how students engage with their learning and interact with each other. This research aims to explore students’ self-regulation learning behavior's particularly on reflection practices via online learning environment by represent qualitative and quantitative analysis. This grant research is under University Science of Malaysia (USM) and Dr. Siti Khadijah Binti Mohamad as project leader for this research.




INTRODUCTION

Monitoring

Monitoring online learning behavior is essential for maintaining a high standard of education, supporting student success, and continuously improving the learning experience.Improving the effectiveness of your teaching methods can significantly enhance student learning and engagement.

Enhancing self-regulation

Enhancing self-regulation in online learning is vital for students to manage their own learning processes effectively. By incorporating strategies such as goal setting, time management, and self-assessment, learners can take greater ownership of their educational journey. Providing tools and resources for tracking progress and reflecting on their performance helps students develop the skills needed to stay motivated, organized, and proactive in their learning endeavors.

Ensuring educational effectiveness

Monitoring online learning is crucial for ensuring educational effectiveness and student engagement. By keeping a close eye on progress and interactions, educators can identify areas where learners may need additional support, adapt instructional strategies to meet diverse needs, and foster a more personalized and impactful learning experience.

THE IMPORTANCE AND BENEFITS OF RESEARCH

THRIVING IS ALL ABOUT WORKING ON YOUR SELF-DEVELOPMENT

The profiling of students' self-regulated learning behaviors can be used by educators to improve self-regulation learning instructions and provide training for students using different strategies. This is particularly important in online learning settings where reflective practices can be supported effectively. Learning analytics methods can help students regulate their learning process and improve their reflective practice, ultimately leading to more successful outcomes. The findings of students' self-regulated learning behavior profiles can also be used to develop data-driven software for online teaching and learning, addressing challenges in the education sector.

Finally, the self-regulated learning behavioural profile in online environment can be replicated and validated by other researchers for other groups of higher education samples and through different online learning settings in future research.

Virtual training systems

An efficient virtual training system refers to a platform or software that effectively delivers training and educational content in a virtual or online setting. Key aspects of an efficient virtual training system include user-friendly interfaces, interactive elements, personalized learning options, reliable performance analytics, collaboration tools, scalability, and flexibility to adapt to different learning needs and objectives. Such a system aims to enhance engagement, learning outcomes, and overall effectiveness of virtual training initiatives.

Online remote monitoring

Online remote monitoring refers to the process of monitoring and supervising activities, processes, or systems from a remote location using internet-connected devices or technology. This practice allows individuals or organizations to track and gather data in real-time without physically being present at the location of interest.

Web Class Analysis

Web class analysis refers to the evaluation and assessment of online courses or educational programs delivered through the internet. It involves analyzing various aspects of the web-based learning experience, such as content quality, user engagement, learning outcomes, student performance, and overall effectiveness of the virtual instruction. By examining these factors, educators and administrators can identify strengths, weaknesses, and opportunities for improvement in online teaching and learning environments.

Reflection can take two forms, namely (1) self-reflection which occurs when learners reflect upon their own learning by taking into consideration of their personal opinions, experiences, beliefs and assertions, and (2) reflection upon other material which happens when they reflect on others’ opinions, experiences, beliefs, assertions and also factual knowledge presented in the content.

SAMPLE CLASS

The data to run this proposed research will be gathered from a cohort of second-year pre-service teachers at school of educational studies in USM majoring in Teachers of English to Speakers of Other Languages (TESOL) programme with a minor in educational technology who enroll in Digital Audio and Video Production course. This course will be taught by DR SITI NAZLEEN ABDUL RABU from PTPM USM.

Web Analytics Tools & Analyzing Benefit

Web analytics refers to the measurement, collection, analysis, and reporting of web data to understand and optimize the usage of a website. It involves tracking various metrics and user interactions on a website to assess its performance, user behavior, and effectiveness in achieving business goals.Since strength analytic tools is on website so classroom by website based will be proposed in this research.

Web analytics tools provide insights into key performance indicators (KPIs) such as website traffic, user demographics, click-through rates, conversion rates, bounce rates, and more. By analyzing these metrics, website owners and marketers can make informed decisions to improve the user experience, optimize conversion rates, enhance online marketing strategies, and ultimately achieve their objectives. For this research HOTJAR and GOOGLE ANALYTIC will used as analysis tools.

Web analytics helps in identifying areas for improvement, understanding user preferences, and measuring the success of digital marketing campaigns.
